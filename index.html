welcome to varanasi

The city's largest restaurant seating 500 covers amidst a grand atrium. Set on 3 floors

Featuring a spectacle of opulent surroundings with several private dining rooms and 3 cocktail bars. Our fine dining menu brings forth a new age dining experience within the haute Indian cuisine sector.

Menu spearheaded by top chefs in the industry.

The owner of Varanasi travelled the globe researching the finest bars and restaurants,

from its interior design, how they operate, to how the food is created and presented. Varanasi is the spiritual embodiment of his journey, resulting in the creation of something Birmingham has not seen before.

The team have strived to create an experience like no other. This is also reflected in the luxurious and creative food menu with the hand crafted decor to match that ethos.
